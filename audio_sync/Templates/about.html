How Audio Synchronization works?

Audio Fingerprint

Audio fingerprinting is the process of representing an audio signal in a compact way by extracting relevant features of the audio content. Short summary of audio object using a limited number of bits.

Mel Frequency Cepstral Coefficients(MFCC) 

MFCC features represent units of sound as the shape of the vocal tract.  Simply saying, it represents sound signal into how people hear musical tones. Mel scale is a scale that scales the frequency in order to match more closely what the human ear can hear. MFCCs are a feature widely used in automatic speech and speaker recognition.

Music Onset

Music onset is the detection of the instant when a discrete event begins in music signal. It is also described as finding musically meaningful events in audio signals.

Dynamic Time warping

DTW is used to calculate the distance matrix between two time series of different length. The idea to compare arrays with different length is to build one-to-many and many-to-one matches so that the total distance can be minimized between the two.
